Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 24, 2024 | Last Updated: November 24, 2024

Welcome to Sugam Pokharel's portfolio website ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services, including the AI chat assistant and contact form.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using our website, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the site.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you interact with our website, we may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Form: Name, email address, subject, and message content when you submit the contact form
  • AI Chat Assistant: Messages and conversations you have with our AI-powered chatbot
  • Resume Download: We may log when you download the resume PDF (but do not collect personal information from this action)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing behavior:

  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type and version, device type, operating system, pages visited, time and date of visit, time spent on pages
  • Cookies and Local Storage: Theme preferences, cursor trail settings, and other UI preferences stored in your browser's local storage
  • Log Data: Server logs may record your activity on the website for debugging and performance monitoring purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries submitted through the contact form
  • AI Chat: To provide you with AI-powered assistance about skills, projects, and experience via the chatbot
  • Website Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, improve website functionality, and enhance user experience
  • Technical Maintenance: To monitor and maintain the website's performance and security
  • Personalization: To remember your preferences (e.g., theme, cursor trail settings) across sessions

3. AI Chat Assistant

Our website features an AI-powered chat assistant that uses Google's Gemini AI API. When you use this feature:

  • Your chat messages are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing and response generation
  • Conversations are not permanently stored on our servers but may be temporarily cached for performance
  • Google's privacy policy and data practices apply to the AI processing. You can review Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Chat history is stored in your browser's session and is not shared with third parties

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use browser Local Storage (not traditional cookies) to enhance your experience:

  • Theme Preference: Stores your choice of light or dark mode
  • Cursor Trail Setting: Remembers whether you have enabled or disabled the cursor trail effect

You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings. Disabling Local Storage may affect website functionality and personalization.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Gemini AI: Powers the AI chat assistant (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • Email Service Provider: For sending contact form submissions and confirmation emails
  • Font Libraries: Google Fonts for typography (may collect IP address)
  • Icon Libraries: Font Awesome CDN for icons

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Security headers (X-Frame-Options, X-Content-Type-Options, etc.)
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse and spam
  • Input validation and sanitization to prevent XSS and injection attacks

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Data Retention

  • Contact Form Submissions: We retain contact form data for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and maintain business records
  • AI Chat Conversations: Temporarily stored in browser session only; not permanently retained by us
  • Log Data: Server logs are retained for 30 days for security and debugging purposes

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Objection: Object to our processing of your personal data
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at sugampokharel28@gmail.com.
